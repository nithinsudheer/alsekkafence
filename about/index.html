Our Story

AL SEKKA FENCE LLC Oman was established in January 2025 as a manufacturer of high-quality fencing solutions. We specialize in GI and PVC coated chainlink fence mesh and barbed wires. With a production capacity ranging from 2000 to 4000 meters per day, we ensure timely delivery to our clients across Oman.

We can manufacture chainlink fence mesh up to 3.0 meters, with wire sizes ranging from 1.6mm to 4.0mm. Our commitment to 100% professional work ensures customer satisfaction and long-term reliability.
