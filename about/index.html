Our Story

Established with a vision to provide superior fencing solutions, Al Sekka Fence has grown to become one of Oman's most trusted manufacturers of chainlink fences, barbed wire, and mesh panels. With over 40 years of manufacturing excellence, we've built our reputation on quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction.

Our state-of-the-art facility in Al Rusayl Industrial Area, Muscat, serves as the hub for our operations across the GCC region. We combine traditional craftsmanship with modern technology to deliver products that meet the highest international standards.
